Your Consumer Rights

As a UK-based business, we comply with UK consumer protection laws:

  • Right to cancel: Under UK law, you have 14 days to cancel digital content purchases, BUT this right is lost once you download or access the content
  • Quality standards: Products must be as described - we ensure all materials match our descriptions
  • Faulty digital content: If content is faulty or doesn't match the description, we'll provide a remedy

UK Consumer Rights: By downloading our digital products immediately after purchase, you acknowledge that your right to cancel under the Consumer Contracts Regulations is lost.
