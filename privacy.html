Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how VersaDigital collects, uses, and protects your personal information in plain English - no confusing legal jargon.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Email address - When you take our ADHD quiz or make a purchase
  • Quiz responses - To determine your ADHD type and provide personalized recommendations
  • Purchase information - Name and payment details processed securely through Gumroad
  • Communication data - When you email us for support

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide your quiz results and personalized ADHD type recommendations
  • Deliver digital products you've purchased
  • Send follow-up emails with helpful ADHD tips and strategies
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Improve our products and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We never sell your data. We only share information in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers - Gumroad for payment processing, ConvertKit for email delivery
  • Legal requirements - If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business transfers - In the event of a merger or sale of our business

Your Rights (GDPR & UK GDPR)

As a UK-based business, we comply with data protection laws. You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification - Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure - Request deletion of your data
  • Portability - Receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent - Unsubscribe from emails at any time
  • Object - Object to processing for marketing purposes

Data Security

We protect your information using:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with reputable providers
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal data (only Lawrence has access)

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking:

  • Essential cookies - For website functionality
  • Analytics - To understand how visitors use our site (anonymized)
  • No advertising cookies - We don't use cookies for ads or cross-site tracking

Data Retention

We keep your information:

  • Quiz data - Until you request deletion
  • Purchase records - 7 years for tax purposes
  • Email data - Until you unsubscribe
  • Support communications - 2 years for quality assurance

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you're a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside the UK/EU by our service providers (ConvertKit in the US, Gumroad globally). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place through:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Service provider certifications (Privacy Shield successors)

Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy or your data? Contact us:

  • Email: lawrence@versadigital.co
  • Response time: Within 72 hours
  • Address: VersaDigital, England & Wales

Changes to this policy: We'll update this page and notify you via email if we make significant changes to how we handle your data.
